ABOUT US

The Zelf Karte Team

We, the students of International institute of Technoogy,Hyderabad have put in our efforts towards creating a revolutionary change in the traditional self-service shopping stores.Our high-end technology offers a cashless digital shopping platform where everything you buy adds up to your digital cart on a scan and payment is done online.

We would like to introduce you all to our team which is headed by MR.ABHISHEK CHAWLA Other members are Pragya, Pranjal, Samruddhi, Gunjan, Kartik, Dinesh, Yash.




As the name suggests: We have worked towards creating a platform where the customer will not have to wait in long billing queues any more. They simply have to choose their items, pick them up, pay through the Zelf Karte app and go back home with their stuff :))

This is a great opportunity for the shopkeepers to keep their shops open even in this situation of world-pandemic without having to sit at the shop for the whole time.

OUR STORE

Here are how Zelf Karte stores looks like...


Any Zelf-Karte store contains a number of vending machines with multiple outlets for the customers. The customers need to enter the quantity of the product, scan the qr-code and pay.....The vending machines will automatically deliver their products. The store is divided into various sections with similar items grouped together.
